Real estate professionals are increasingly using AI to cut the time spent on repetitive tasks: writing listing descriptions, following up with leads, creating social media posts, and analyzing market data. The tools in this section are reviewed specifically for solo agents, small teams, and brokerages — not enterprise tech buyers.

We test each tool against real estate workflows: do listing descriptions sound natural and accurate, not obviously AI-generated? Does the CRM actually save time on follow-up? Can a non-technical agent use it without ongoing IT support?

**What you'll find here:**

- **Listing description generators** — tools that produce accurate, market-ready property copy
- **Lead nurturing platforms** — CRMs with AI follow-up sequences and predictive scoring
- **Market analysis tools** — automated CMAs and neighborhood data summarizers
- **Social media automation** — AI that creates and schedules property posts without manual effort

Every tool is reviewed hands-on. Ratings cover output quality, real-world ease of use, and whether the price is fair for a solo agent or small team.
