## Alex Turner — Lead Reviewer & Editor

Alex Turner has spent the last 6 years working at the intersection of technology and content creation. After building his first SaaS product in 2019, he became obsessed with the tools that help creators and small businesses move faster — and which ones are just hype.

He started ToolCritic in 2024 after realizing that most AI tool "reviews" were just rephrased marketing copy. The site's mission: test everything hands-on, rate honestly, and never let affiliate money influence a score.

**Areas of expertise:**
- AI writing and content generation tools
- SEO and content marketing automation
- AI video and image generation
- Productivity software for creators and freelancers

**Methodology:** Every tool reviewed on ToolCritic is tested with real use cases — generating actual content, running real workflows, and measuring time saved. Ratings are based on output quality, pricing fairness, ease of use, and reliability over time.

**Disclosure:** ToolCritic participates in affiliate programs. Some links on this site earn a small commission when you subscribe to a tool — at no extra cost to you. Affiliate relationships never influence ratings or recommendations. A tool with no affiliate program gets the same honest treatment as one that pays 40% commission.
