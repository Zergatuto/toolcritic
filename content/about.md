## What is ToolCritic?

ToolCritic is an independent review platform dedicated to testing and comparing AI tools for creators, freelancers, and small businesses. We launched in 2024 after noticing that most AI tool "reviews" were either thinly disguised marketing copy or listicles written by people who had never opened the product.

Our position is simple: we only publish reviews based on hands-on testing with real workflows. No filler, no hype, no scores that move when affiliate commissions change.

## Who Writes the Reviews

Our lead reviewer and editor is **[Alex Turner](/author/)**, who has spent six years working at the intersection of technology and content creation. He tests every tool personally, using real use cases — not demos, trials padded with screenshots, or secondhand reports.

## How We Review

### Testing Process

Every tool is tested through a consistent workflow before we write a single word:

1. We sign up for a real account (free tier first, then paid if the free tier is too restricted to evaluate the product meaningfully)
2. We run the tool against a fixed set of real-world tasks relevant to the tool's target user — not cherry-picked demos
3. We test for at least one full work session before forming conclusions
4. For audio/video tools, we use our own recordings. For writing tools, we test on real content briefs

### Rating Criteria

All tools are rated on the same five dimensions, each weighted by how much it matters for the tool's primary use case:

| Dimension | What we measure |
|-----------|----------------|
| Output quality | Does the tool actually produce good results? |
| Price vs. value | Is the cost justified for what you get? |
| Ease of use | Time to first useful output |
| Reliability | Consistency across multiple sessions |
| Support & updates | Is the product actively maintained? |

Our overall score is a weighted average on a 1–10 scale. A 7 means a solid tool with real limitations. A 9 means we'd recommend it without hesitation to most users in the target audience. We have never given a 10.

### Independence

Affiliate relationships do not influence ratings. A tool with an affiliate program gets the same honest treatment as one that pays nothing. We have published negative reviews of tools we're affiliated with, and positive reviews of tools with no affiliate program at all. If a score ever changed because of commercial pressure, this site would have no reason to exist.

All affiliate relationships are disclosed per-article. If a review contains an affiliate link, it is labeled at the bottom of the article.

### Update Policy

Pricing and feature information is verified at the time of publication. AI tools change fast — plans get restructured, features get removed, pricing tiers shift. We update articles when we become aware of significant changes. The publication and last-modified dates on each article reflect when the content was most recently verified.

## What We Don't Do

- We don't accept sponsored reviews or paid placements
- We don't publish "top 10" lists based on which tools have affiliate programs
- We don't review tools we haven't personally tested
