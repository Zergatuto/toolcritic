## What is ToolCritic?

ToolCritic is an independent review platform dedicated to testing and comparing AI tools for creators, freelancers, and small businesses.

We believe choosing the wrong AI tool wastes time and money. Our mission is simple: we do the testing so you can make confident decisions.

## What We Review

- **AI Writing Tools** — content creation, copywriting, SEO writing
- **AI Image Generators** — for designers, marketers, and creators
- **AI Video Tools** — editing, generation, and automation
- **AI Productivity Tools** — automation, scheduling, research
- **AI for Business** — customer service, sales, analytics

## How We Review

Every tool on ToolCritic is evaluated on:

1. **Real output quality** — we generate actual samples, not just read feature lists
2. **Price vs. value** — is it worth the subscription?
3. **Ease of use** — how long does it take to get results?
4. **Affiliate disclosure** — some links on this site are affiliate links. We earn a small commission if you subscribe through our link, at no extra cost to you. This never influences our ratings.
